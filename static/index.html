What's Jackett?
Most torrent sites block automated searches — they'll hit you with CAPTCHAs, Cloudflare walls, or just ban your IP. Jackett handles all of that. It talks to 100+ torrent sites and feeds the results back to Hydra.

Hydra is the app you use. Jackett is the thing that goes out and finds torrents without getting blocked.

You don't need it to use Hydra. But if you want more results, more sources, and more peers — add Jackett.
